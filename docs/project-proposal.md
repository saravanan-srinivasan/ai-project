# Project Proposal: AI-Powered Digital Legacy Manager

## Title

AI-Powered Digital Legacy Manager: Intelligent Preservation, Organization, and Inheritance of Personal Digital Assets

## Abstract

The AI-Powered Digital Legacy Manager is a centralized intelligent platform designed to discover, analyze, organize, preserve, and securely transfer personal digital assets. It uses artificial intelligence, natural language processing, optical character recognition, facial recognition, retrieval-augmented generation, and large language models to transform fragmented digital information into a structured digital estate.

The system addresses a growing challenge in modern society: valuable personal, financial, legal, and emotional assets are scattered across many digital services and devices. When an individual dies, becomes incapacitated, or loses access to accounts, families may struggle to locate documents, identify assets, manage subscriptions, retrieve memories, and understand the person's digital presence.

## Background

Modern individuals generate enormous digital footprints. These include:

- Personal photographs and videos
- Emails and messages
- Social media activity
- Cloud storage files
- Financial records
- Insurance policies
- Property documents
- Investment portfolios
- Subscription receipts
- Digital wallet records
- Medical documents
- Legal and estate records

Although these assets have emotional, financial, historical, and legal value, they are usually fragmented across accounts, devices, and platforms. Existing storage and password management tools do not understand or organize the meaning of this data.

## Problem Definition

After a person's death or incapacitation, families often face several practical problems:

- Important legal and financial documents are difficult to locate.
- Bank accounts, insurance policies, and investments may remain undiscovered.
- Active subscriptions may continue deducting money.
- Family photographs and memories may be lost.
- Beneficiaries may not know which accounts or assets exist.
- Manual review of emails, folders, and documents can take months.

The lack of an intelligent, unified digital estate management system creates emotional stress, financial loss, and administrative difficulty.

## Objectives

The project aims to:

- Consolidate digital assets from multiple authorized data sources.
- Automatically classify documents, images, emails, and financial records.
- Extract key information using OCR and NLP.
- Generate concise summaries of important documents.
- Detect recurring subscriptions, assets, liabilities, and policies.
- Reconstruct important personal memories into a timeline.
- Provide natural language retrieval over the digital estate.
- Generate secure inheritance reports for authorized beneficiaries.

## Proposed System

The proposed platform securely connects to data sources such as Gmail, Outlook, Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, local storage devices, social media exports, and document repositories. It ingests files, extracts metadata, performs OCR where needed, classifies content, indexes text, creates embeddings, and stores structured asset records.

AI components analyze documents and communications to identify meaningful entities such as account numbers, policy holders, nominees, maturity dates, property identifiers, payment cycles, recurring subscriptions, family members, locations, and event dates.

Beneficiaries or authorized users can search the digital estate through a dashboard or conversational interface. The system can also generate a secure inheritance package containing asset summaries, document links, account inventories, financial obligations, emergency contacts, and memory archives.

## Functional Requirements

### Asset Discovery

- Connect to authorized email, cloud, and local storage sources.
- Ingest files and metadata.
- Detect duplicates and related files.
- Classify documents by type and importance.

### Document Intelligence

- Extract text from PDFs, scans, images, and forms.
- Summarize financial, legal, insurance, property, and medical documents.
- Extract entities, dates, amounts, nominee names, account references, and obligations.

### Memory Preservation

- Organize photographs and events chronologically.
- Use metadata, OCR, and facial recognition to group memories.
- Generate meaningful life-event timelines.

### Financial and Subscription Detection

- Analyze bank statements, emails, and receipts.
- Detect recurring payments and subscription services.
- Identify investments, premiums, loans, liabilities, and asset records.

### Inheritance Reporting

- Generate role-based inheritance packages.
- Separate sensitive financial data from family memory archives.
- Support exportable reports for beneficiaries and legal use.

### Conversational Search

- Support natural language queries over indexed assets.
- Retrieve source-backed answers using RAG.
- Provide citations or links to original files where possible.

## Non-Functional Requirements

- Strong encryption for files, metadata, and reports.
- Role-based access control for beneficiaries.
- Multi-factor authentication.
- Full audit logging.
- Privacy-preserving processing for sensitive records.
- Scalable ingestion and search.
- Clear consent and authorization workflows.
- Secure deletion and retention policies.

## AI Components

- OCR for scanned documents and images.
- NLP for classification, summarization, and entity extraction.
- LLMs for document explanation and question answering.
- RAG for source-grounded conversational retrieval.
- Face recognition for organizing personal photographs.
- Clustering and metadata analysis for timeline generation.

## Novelty

The novelty of the system lies in combining digital estate planning, AI-based document understanding, memory reconstruction, asset discovery, financial detection, and conversational retrieval into a unified platform. Existing products usually focus on password management, cloud backup, or document storage separately.

## Expected Outcomes

- A centralized digital estate repository.
- Searchable and categorized personal assets.
- Summaries of important documents.
- Detected financial accounts, subscriptions, and obligations.
- A personal memory timeline.
- Secure beneficiary access workflows.
- Exportable digital inheritance reports.

## Conclusion

The AI-Powered Digital Legacy Manager offers a practical and socially meaningful approach to digital estate management. It helps families preserve personal history, locate critical assets, reduce financial confusion, and responsibly transfer digital wealth and memories across generations.
